You have heard my prayer, Lord,
and blessed me with this child.
Her hair is as unruly as her spirit is wild.
Help her build your kingdom
and keep her from all harm.
Protect her from the evil here
and far beyond this barb.
Help her become the vision
that I can never be.
Help her become a better, stronger, wiser.
Happier.
My poor mother, she had no idea how her prayers were about to be answered.
